Company overview

Local stores for hard-to-find anime collectibles.

One-Punch Anime Emporium is a small anime retailer with four locations in the greater Chico, California area. The company is known for rare figures, comic books, manga, and a loyal customer base built through local advertising and word of mouth.

The Need

Customer surveys showed that many shoppers wanted to see available products online, search current inventory, order items remotely, and request special-order products without visiting every store in person.

The Solution

The new website provides browsing, category pages, inventory-style product cards, order submission, store locations, contact information, and a dedicated special-order request form.

Approach

Built with a simple SDLC process.

The team planned the site map, created a static storefront, tested navigation and forms, and prepared deployment through Cloudflare Pages.

Planning

Identify customer needs and required pages.

Design

Create a clear page hierarchy and consistent storefront layout.

Implementation

Build, test, deploy, and document the final site.
