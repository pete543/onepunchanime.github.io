Company overview

Local stores for hard-to-find anime collectibles.

One-Punch Anime Emporium is a small anime retailer with four locations in the greater Chico, California area. The company is known for rare figures, comic books, manga, and a loyal customer base built through local advertising and word of mouth.

Why Shop Online

Customer surveys showed that many shoppers wanted to see available products online, search current inventory, order items remotely, and request special-order products without visiting every store in person.

What You Can Do

Browse featured stock, check product categories, submit a pickup request, find store locations, contact staff, and request rare items through the special-order form.
